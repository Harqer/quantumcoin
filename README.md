# QuantumCoin

QuantumCoin is an AI-powered financial assistant and budgeting application, bringing intuitive, conversational money management to the blockchain era. Inspired by apps like Cleo AI, QuantumCoin helps users effortlessly track spending, stick to budgets, and manage their finances through a friendly, intelligent chat interface—with the added security of quantum-safe cryptographic principles.

## Features

- **AI Money Coach**: Chat directly with your financial assistant to get instant insights into your spending habits and account balances.
- **Automated Budgeting**: Simplify complex financial data into easy-to-understand language and actionable advice.
- **Quantum-Safe Backend**: Integrated with post-quantum cryptography (PQC) and hardware-backed Quantum Random Number Generators (QNRG) to ensure your financial data and assets are secure against both classical and future quantum threats.
- **Seamless Integrations**: Connects with modern financial APIs and tools for real-time tracking of assets, liabilities, and crypto holdings.
